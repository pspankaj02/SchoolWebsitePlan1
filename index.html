About Us

Springfield High School is dedicated to nurturing the intellectual and personal growth of every student. Founded in 1985, our school boasts a diverse curriculum, outstanding staff, and a supportive community.

  • Established: 1985
  • Grades: 6-12
  • Accreditation: State Board Certified

  • Arts and Sports
